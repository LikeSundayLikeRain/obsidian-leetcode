<objective>
Refactor `ProblemBrowserView`'s row-click handler into a `routeProblemClick(slug, status, intent, opts?)` method on `LeetCodePlugin`, add the `previewClickBehavior` field on `PluginData`, and add a new "Preview" settings section with a `Click behavior` dropdown. This avoids the "scavenger hunt" anti-pattern in Plan 06-03 — the executor for the view receives an already-wired router and only needs to swap the placeholder Notice for the real `openOrReusePreview` call.

Output: `routeProblemClick` method on LeetCodePlugin; new `previewClickBehavior: 'preview' | 'open'` field on PluginData (default `'preview'`); new "Preview" section in the settings tab with a dropdown; updated row-click handler in ProblemBrowserView; three test files asserting the click-behavior matrix and the SettingsStore round-trip.
