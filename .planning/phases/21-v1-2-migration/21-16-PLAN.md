- Step 6 (`vault.create`) writes the bytes to disk.

Even though both writes complete BEFORE step 10 in the sequential async flow (`await` chains), there is a metadataCache propagation latency: `processFrontMatter` writes the frontmatter atomically, but `metadataCache.getFileCache(file)?.frontmatter` may return a stale snapshot (or undefined) for several macrotasks afterward. The widget mount path at `WidgetController.resolveLanguageSlug` (per CLAUDE.md the "lc-language frontmatter missing; falling back to Python" Notice fires from there) reads the frontmatter from metadataCache at MOUNT time.
