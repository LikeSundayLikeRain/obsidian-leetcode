<objective>
Close UAT Gap 1 (Test 1, severity=minor): "After auto-migration in Reading mode, the v1.3 widget does not mount on the first open — the user must close and reopen the note." (per 21-HUMAN-UAT.md lines 88-110, root cause confirmed at lines 93-96).

Purpose: Reading mode has no equivalent of CM6 ViewPlugin's reactive update cycle. After `migrateLegacyFenceIfNeeded` rewrites the fence opener via `vault.process`, no mechanism asks Obsidian's preview to re-run post-processors. The plugin's `vault.on('modify')` handler at `src/main.ts:1226-1249` short-circuits when no widget is registered (always true for legacy fences in Reading mode pre-migration). This plan adds an explicit post-migration `previewMode.rerender(true)` call gated on `migrated === true`.

Output: `src/main/readingModeMigrationHook.ts` extended to walk `app.workspace.getLeavesOfType('markdown')`, filter to leaves whose `view.file?.path === file.path` AND `view.getMode() === 'preview'`, and call `view.previewMode.rerender(true)` after the migration promise resolves with `migrated === true`. New unit tests assert the rerender call is made/skipped under each branch (migrated=true, migrated=false, rejection, non-preview leaf, mismatched path).
</objective>
