## Open follow-ups (deferred to next gap-closure cycle)

1. **G-DROPDOWN-CLIPPED** — chevron dropdown is clipped by `cm-content`'s `contain: paint`, making dropdown items unhittable. z-index 9999 + isolation didn't fix it because `contain: paint` overrides stacking rules for hit testing. **Required fix:** portal the dropdown to `document.body` with absolute positioning derived from the chevron button's `getBoundingClientRect()`. ~30-60 min refactor, including outside-click and scroll-tracking lifecycle. Tests at `tests/main/languageChevronWidget.test.ts` need updates for the portal pattern.

2. **G-PICKER-MODAL-NOCLOSE-ON-COPY** — `SubmissionDetailModal` (inner) closes on copy success, but the outer `SubmissionPickerModal` stays open. User expected BOTH to dismiss. **Required fix:** add `onSuccess` callback to `SubmissionDetailDeps`; picker subscribes and closes itself. Decoupling discipline forbids direct imports between the two modals — wiring must flow through deps/callbacks. Small fix once the callback path is added.

Both are documented in detail in `05.3-UAT.md` `## Open follow-ups (deferred to next gap-closure cycle — DO NOT LOSE)`.
